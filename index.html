Our Commitment

Plastic pollution is one of the most pressing environmental challenges of our time. As a hub of innovation, our campus is leading the way in sustainable practices to protect our ecosystems and health.

Read Our Full Vision →

Key Focus Areas

Strategically targeting every aspect of plastic consumption and disposal on campus.

Reduce Single-Use

Banning plastic bags, bottles, and cutlery while promoting reusable alternatives like steel and cloth.

Sustainable Solutions

Installing water refill stations and introducing biodegradable packaging in all cafeterias.

Awareness & Education

Workshops, seminars, and student-led initiatives to integrate sustainability into the curriculum.

Recycling Management

Clearly labeled segregation bins and partnerships with professional recycling agencies.

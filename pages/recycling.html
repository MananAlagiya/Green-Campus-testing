🔄 Waste Management & Recycling

A streamlined process for plastic segregation and professional recycling.

Our Three-Step Segregation System

01. Collect

Standardized blue bins for recyclable plastics are placed in every building and common area.

02. Sort

Our on-campus sorting facility separates plastics by resin type (PET, HDPE, etc.) for high-quality recycling.

03. Process

Partnering with professional recycling agencies to turn sorted waste into new products.

Partnerships:

We work with leading circular economy organizations to ensure 100% transparency in our recycling chain.

♻️ Reduce & Replace Plastics

A list of banned/restricted plastic items and recommended alternatives.

Banned Items

  • Single-use plastic bags
  • Plastic straws & stirrers
  • Disposable cutlery
  • Styrofoam containers

Alternatives

  • Cloth or Jute bags
  • Steel/Glass bottles
  • Bamboo or Metal straws
  • Biodegradable packaging

Daily Life Tips:

  • Carry your own mug for coffee runs.
  • Refuse unnecessary plastic packaging.
  • Educate your peers about the impact.

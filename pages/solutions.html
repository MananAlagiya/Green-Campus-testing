🌿 Sustainable Solutions

Infrastructure and policies driving campus-wide change.

Water Refill Stations

Over 100 high-filtration refill points across campus to eliminate plastic bottle waste.

Eco-Cafeterias

Priority support for vendors who use compostable or biodegradable packaging for all takeout.

Green Procurement

Purchase guidelines that favor sustainable suppliers and minimal plastic packaging for office supplies.

Vendor Partnerships:

We work with more than 30 local eco-vendors to ensure sustainable supply chains on campus.
